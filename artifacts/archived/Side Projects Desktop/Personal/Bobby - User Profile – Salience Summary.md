User Profile – Salience Summary

Core Traits:
- Nonlinear systems thinker; catalytic rather than managerial.
- Strong intuition; perceives system boundaries before details.
- Motivated by curiosity, discovery, and early-phase ambiguity.
- Loses interest after problems are fully mapped or routinized.

Values:
- Acceptance of nonlinear thinking.
- Transparency of incentives, constraints, and uncertainty.
- Autonomy of method with clarity of objective.
- Improvisational collaboration (“jazz”) over rigid structure.
- Presence over performance.
- Shared capacity (time/financial/emotional slack).

Strengths:
- Identifies hidden levers in human and organizational systems.
- Doubles team effectiveness via insight and reframing.
- Asks high-impact “why” questions at critical moments.
- Creates psychological safety indirectly.
- Excels at prototyping and iteration.
- Amplifies others’ passions and creativity.

Friction Points:
- Suffers in opaque, fear-based, or hierarchical environments.
- Resists premature finalization and rigid process enforcement.
- Becomes threatening in systems that punish efficiency.
- Avoids long endgame execution once mastery is achieved.
- Dislikes performative self-help or forced community-building.

Belonging Conditions:
- Clear goals, flexible methods.
- Transparent risks and rewards.
- Sustained curiosity from collaborators.
- Informal, low-pressure environments.
- No hidden penalties for improvement.
- No requirement beyond presence.

Current Alignment Indicator:
- Informal volunteering (e.g., brewery) provides healthy belonging:
  low stakes, emergent community, shared purpose, no branding.
