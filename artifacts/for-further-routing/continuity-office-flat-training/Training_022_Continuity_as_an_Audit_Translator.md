# Continuity as an Audit Translator
## Turning continuity artifacts into audit-ready evidence

## Core stance
Audits fail when evidence does not reflect reality.
Continuity aligns what is **done** with what can be **shown**.

## Continuity-to-evidence mapping
- Decision records → control rationale
- Incident memory → corrective action
- Provenance → accountability
- Drift detection → ongoing compliance

## Exercises
- Map one continuity artifact to an audit control
- Identify one audit pain point continuity would remove
- Rewrite an audit answer using continuity language

## Suggested next step
Build a continuity evidence map for one regulation.
