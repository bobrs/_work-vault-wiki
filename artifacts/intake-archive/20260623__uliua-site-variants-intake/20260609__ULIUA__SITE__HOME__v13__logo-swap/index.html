Attractor Glyphs + keyboard shortcuts

The attractor glyphs are small symbolic seeds. Type the attractor keystroke, let your keyboard replacement expand it, then use the glyph as a signal, shirt mark, sticker, post, or conversation doorway.

Mini-howto

Type the attractor. Let the glyph appear.

Create a text replacement on your device: the shortcut starts with a semicolon, like ;consent.
Set the replacement text to the glyph, or to the lockup you want to wear and share: 🝁 or 🝁 ;consent.
Use the keystroke when invoking the attractor. Use the glyph when you want the signal to travel.
