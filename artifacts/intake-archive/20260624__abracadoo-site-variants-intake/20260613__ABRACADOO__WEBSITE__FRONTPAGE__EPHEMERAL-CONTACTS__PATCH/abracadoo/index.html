What works today

A small, useful beginning.

The current Abracadoo MVP creates local HumanKey acquaintances using Authenticator-compatible TOTP tokens. It is intentionally simple: create, share, recognize, and keep your trust material local.

That means the public site can carry the mythic invitation while the app remains calm, legible, and security-adjacent. The front page is the promise. The app is the lantern.
