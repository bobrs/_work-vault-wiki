Intelligence scales safely only when consent scales with it.

The internet proved that communication can be engineered at planetary scale. Artificial intelligence is proving that cognition, persuasion, delegation, and coordination can scale as well.

But when intelligence scales without consent, coordination becomes extraction. Systems learn to capture attention, infer preferences, compress people into profiles, and convert ambiguity into permission.

Consentful Cybernetics asks a different question: how do we build systems that preserve agency, refusal, scope, revocation, context, and repair as intelligence becomes more powerful?

Research program

From moral intuition to measurable system property.

The research program begins from a strong hypothesis: consent is not merely a legal formality or interpersonal courtesy. It may be a measurable stability property across living, technical, organizational, and multi-agent systems.

Current preregistered studies examine consent as stability, consent as an explicit entropy-reducing state transition, consent as a cross-substrate invariant, and waste as a lagging signal of unnoticed consent collapse.

Model layer

Consent dynamics are trajectories, not tokens.

The model layer includes AI model training, LLM fine-tuning experiments, embedding spaces, and vectorized consent-dynamics data designed to make consent-relevant patterns easier to study, search, reuse, and improve.

A Consent Dynamics Model should not declare “consent happened.” It should help humans and agents notice the surrounding trajectory: power, context, scope, timing, medium, hesitation, refusal-safety, reversibility, drift, repair, and witness.

The long-term goal is a reusable data and model commons: consent-aware training corpora, annotated interaction patterns, vector stores, and reference models that can be used directly or adapted to train other systems, where safety and privacy allow.

Train witnesses, not cops.

Develop LLMs and classifiers that surface uncertainty before harm hardens into breach.

Vectorize without capture.

Create searchable consent-dynamics data artifacts without converting ambiguity into permission.
