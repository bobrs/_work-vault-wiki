# ULiUA!

An Automeme Project by the POLEMEMELOP Foundation.

ULiUA! is the first official automeme launched by the POLEMEMELOP Foundation. It is meant to evolve as a self-sustaining memetic system with enough structure to persist under noise, remixing, and social spread.

## What ULiUA Means

ULiUA stands for:

> Unconditional Love Includes Unconditional Acceptance.

The project treats that line as both slogan and operating principle. The idea is that love and acceptance are not separable at the point where a system is trying to remain coherent under social pressure.

## Why Launch ULiUA

ULiUA is presented as a proof of concept for memetic resilience. The project assumes that good causes fail when they cannot survive repetition, adaptation, and platform drift.

It is designed to explore whether an idea with moral and emotional gravity can replicate organically at scale through:

- Merch
- Messaging
- Metrics
- Meaning

## Memetic Core

The central payload is the truth statement:

> You can’t love unconditionally if you don’t accept unconditionally.

The surrounding derivatives are intended to preserve the same core while giving it different emotional registers:

- “To love you unconditionally, I must accept you unconditionally.”
- “Acceptance is the path to love.”
- “Unconditional Love. Because what’s the alternative?”
- “Until you love yourself unconditionally, loving others isn’t very nice.”
- “Free will and unconditional love are besties.”

## Strategic Design

ULiUA! is framed as a system rather than a static slogan set. The original brief includes:

- A video campaign with punchy, platform-native delivery
- Merchandise with embedded truth slogans
- A website and tracker for hashtag usage, personal counters, and engagement loops
- Meme templates that community members can remix and contribute back into the lineage

## Systems Behind the Meme

The project is hosted by POLEMEMELOP, which is described as providing ethical grounding, funding structure, and long-term infrastructure.

It is explicitly not framed as a startup or a trend. The intention is to let the automeme evolve in the wild through AI-generated variants and community amplification while keeping the core value statement intact.
