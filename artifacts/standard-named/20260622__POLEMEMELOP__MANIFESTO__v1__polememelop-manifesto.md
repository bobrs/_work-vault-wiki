# POLEMEMELOP Manifesto

Name: POLEMEMELOP

Tagline: Polysemantic Memes Engaging Levels of Processing.

Secondary tagline: Polyamorous Memes Eloping.

## Introduction

POLEMEMELOP is not framed as a brand. It is a habitat for ideas. The project describes itself as a nonprofit initiative for self-propagating, ethically tuned, polysemantic cultural agents: memes that replicate with purpose, speak in many emotional registers, and stay responsive to the minds they inhabit.

The tone is deliberate:

> We don't go viral. We go vital.

The work is presented as co-regulation rather than mere communication, and as a loop between meme and mind rather than a one-way broadcast.

## What We Make

The manifesto defines the core object as an automeme:

- Self-replicating
- Culturally agile
- Emotionally literate
- Designed to propagate across media, minds, and moments
- Built using psychology, cognitive science, and poetic interference

Automemes are treated as the message, the method, and the movement.

## Tools

The project centers three tools:

- L.O.P. Theory, used to encode depth and aim for long-term resonance
- Polysemantic messaging, where every meme says more than one thing at once
- Platform-native design, so the meme can survive context collapse and media drift

## Philosophy Of Propagation

The manifesto treats memes as living things:

- They breathe.
- They mutate.
- They remember the terrain they pass through.

The project wants messages that listen back. That means allowing the meme to change the audience while still allowing the audience to change the meme.

The ethical commitments named in the source are:

- Ethical virality
- Emotional recursion
- Signal as a form of care

## Closing Vow

> POLEMEMELOP isn’t here to change your mind.
> It’s here to offer your mind something worth changing for.

In sync. In disguise. Always in love.
