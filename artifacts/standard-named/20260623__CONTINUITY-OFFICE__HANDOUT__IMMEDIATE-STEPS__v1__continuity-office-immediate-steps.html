Continuity lens

Continuity Office preserves five invariants as tools, people, and systems change:

  • Intent persistence
  • Consent continuity
  • Legibility
  • Reversibility
  • Power-proportionate governance
Reminder

Continuity Office does not veto decisions or approve tools. We preserve invariants; leadership decides.

One-sentence test

“If this automation scaled 10×, would we still be able to explain it, constrain it, and roll it back?”

Continuity illuminates consequences. Leadership decides.
