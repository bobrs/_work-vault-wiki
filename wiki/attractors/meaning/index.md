## Semantic Compression

In this vault, meaning is how symbols become consequential: language, story, metaphor, artifact, interpretation, and shared reality becoming able to move bodies, institutions, memories, and futures.

## Why It Matters

Meaning is no longer merely expressive once machines can act on language. It becomes an operational surface, a governance risk, a trust object, and a living field of coordination.
