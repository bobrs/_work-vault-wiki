## Working Read

This is the access-control and governance layer: voting, offline gaming, and resonance gating as patentable system patterns.

Read now as a governance atlas rather than a patent-ops folder. The voting machine branch names an offline trust boundary, the offline-gambling branch names deterministic play without network dependency, and the resonance-gating branch names consent-conditioned access. Together they describe how authority, participation, and system state get gated.

That makes the cluster useful even after the provisional filings are abandoned: the documents still inform the wiki’s governance vocabulary, especially where consent, witness, trust, and revocation need to stay legible.
