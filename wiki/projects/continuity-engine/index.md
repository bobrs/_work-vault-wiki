## Working Read

The client-facing material frames Continuity Engine as an execution layer for governed workflows: it makes procedures visible, enforces structure at runtime, and keeps AI usage inside defined guardrails.

The sovereign governance material uses the same structural language but applies it to Tribal law, commission rules, compact obligations, and regulatory review. That gives the branch a stable two-part shape: one side explains the product, the other side shows where it belongs in authority-heavy environments.
