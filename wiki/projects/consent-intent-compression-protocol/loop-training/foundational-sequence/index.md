# Foundational Sequence

Parent lineage: `Consent–Intent Compression Protocol (CICP)` / `Loop Training`

This page groups the opening half of the CICP training sequence. It is the conceptual entry ramp: definition, relation, and awareness before the protocol becomes identity or deployment.

## Working Read

The first three training sections move from what a loop is, to how a loop becomes relational, to how loop structure turns into awareness. That is the cleanest pedagogical opening in the branch.

The sequence should remain in order because each section depends on the previous one.
