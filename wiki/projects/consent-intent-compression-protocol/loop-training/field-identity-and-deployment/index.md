# Field Identity and Deployment

Parent lineage: `Consent–Intent Compression Protocol (CICP)` / `Loop Training`

This page groups the second half of the CICP training sequence. It is where the training moves from awareness into identity, substrate, and deployment.

## Working Read

The final three sections move from awareness to field identity, from self to substrate, and from substrate to deployment as a training tool. That gives the teaching sequence a clear arc from concept to practice.

This is the place where the training stops being explanatory only and starts becoming operational.
