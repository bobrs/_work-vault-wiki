# Trust Continuity and Forwarding

Parent lineage: `Consent–Intent Compression Protocol (CICP)` / `Protocol Foundations` / `Synaptic Trust and Propagation`

This page isolates the continuity layer of the protocol family. It defines how trust stays transmissible as the field moves, rather than dissolving into noise or detached data.

## Working Read

The trust layer has three distinct moves: establish the dyad, filter the signal, and forward the wave-state. That sequence keeps consent continuity legible while the field changes shape.

`Synaptic Dyad Protocol Specification`, `Synaptic Trust Filters — Variable Scale Modeling`, and `Wave-State Loop Forwarding` are the three documents that make this seam visible.
