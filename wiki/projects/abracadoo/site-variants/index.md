# Site Variants

This seam holds the exploratory homepage and frontpage bundles for `Abracadoo`.

The material is iteration evidence, not a second product line. It shows the public surface being tuned across a series of visual and structural passes, including early hometown/cosmic framing, house-based navigation, deep-green anchoring, app-preview emphasis, copy refinement, and ground-glow tuning.
