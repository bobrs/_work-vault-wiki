## Working Read

This is the navigation and orientation layer for the site family.

Use it for the top-level home page, robots, sitemap, and the grounding entry page.

It is the first grounding stop for readers and tools that need to understand what the site is before they follow the deeper rails.

## Core Claim

The root page is not decoration. It is the smallest public map that explains how to enter the invariant system without confusing presentation with structure.

## Mechanisms

- Surface the home page and navigation before the deeper rails.
- Keep the grounding prompt visible so readers can start from method rather than from a random page.
- Preserve the separation between public site behavior and the machine-readable backing layer.
- Route readers to the spine, identity, and downloads rails when they need structure rather than prose.
