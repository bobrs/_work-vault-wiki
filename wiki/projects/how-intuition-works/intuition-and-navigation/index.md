## Working Read

This page captures the intuition model directly: intuition is treated as a detector, not a source, and movement is used as measurement.

The piece is useful because it names the two modes clearly. Sensing notices field tilt and salience; probing makes small moves to see what responds. That gives the branch a practical vocabulary for navigation without pretending the destination is known in advance.
