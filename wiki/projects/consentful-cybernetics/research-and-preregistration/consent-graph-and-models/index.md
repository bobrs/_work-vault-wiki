# Consent Graph and Models

This seam holds the newer research bundle around consent graphs, loop summary language, protocol introduction, vector mapping, and consent-constrained intelligence.

It sits inside the research track because the documents are trying to formalize consent as a structure rather than as a slogan. The bundle reads like a bridge between the public-facing consent thesis and a more explicit system model.
